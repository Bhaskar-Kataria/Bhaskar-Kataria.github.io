CURRICULUM

The RBP School follows CBSE based curriculum, well integrated value based learning.
The following picture gives the broad outline of subjects and skills covered in our curriculum at different level in order to provide and idea of how a child progress at the RBP School.

CBSE based curriculum with integrated value based learning.

Life Skills & Value Based Learning (The Core): We consider inculcation of values and building life skills as the foundation of all that a child needs to learn life. School ensures that students acquire values and skills to take them through life as a responsible member of family, community and society at large.

Scholastic Skills (The Middle Circle): It covers the content-based subject disciplines i.e. Languages, Humanities, Mathematics, Computers and Sciences. It ensures that students have a good grounding in content across different areas of study.

Co-Scholastic Skills (The Outer Circle): It focuses on developing students' creativity, innovation, interest in sports & games along with grooming their overall personality.

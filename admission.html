ADMISSION PROCEDURE

For Academic year 2019-20 admissions are open for Nursery. Few seats are available in Classes V, VI & VII. There are no seats in KG, II, III & IV.

Age Criteria:

The following Age criteria shall hold for the admissions to Pre-Primary and Primary classes.

Classes Minimum age as on 31st March
(the year in which admission is sought.)

·Play-group ~ 2+ years
·Nursery ~ 3+ years
·K.G. ~ 4+ years
·Class I ~ 5+ years
·Class II ~ 6+ years
·Class III ~ 7+ years
·Class IV ~ 8+ years
·Class V ~ 9+ years 

Documents Required:
·Birth Certificate
·Proof of Residence
·Five Passport size Photographs
·ID proofs of Parents (Aadhar Card)
·TC from previous school (if applicable)

TRANSPORTATION:
Transport is available on advance monthly payment basis for all classes through contract Vans. Amount as approved by school to be paid directly to the transporter.
